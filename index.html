A Montréal-based project manager with a track record in coordinating cross-functional teams, delivering multi-stakeholder initiatives, and driving projects from strategy through execution across private, institutional, and startup environments

I operate at the intersection of project delivery, digital operations, and stakeholder alignment. My work spans end-to-end project coordination, resource planning, and performance tracking for clients and teams across technology, gaming, social economy, and international markets

My approach combines structured planning with adaptive execution: I think about deliverables, dependencies, and stakeholder needs before I think about tools. Whether managing a product launch timeline, coordinating deliverables across distributed teams, or aligning internal and external partners around shared milestones, I focus on what keeps projects moving, on scope, and on time

I've spent the last several years building one consistent skill set through different contexts: knowing how to break complex work into clear plans, coordinate the right people at the right time, and deliver results that meet both the brief and the deadline. That's what project management actually is.

Most people rush to action because it feels productive. But the real leverage is in those quiet hours before anything begins, when mapping the terrain, questioning assumptions, searching for the angle nobody else considered.

That's where great work is born.

What I've learned is that most project failures are actually alignment failures. Teams don't struggle to do the work. They struggle to agree on what success looks like, who owns what, and what the real priorities are. I help organizations answer those three questions before anything gets built.

II'm drawn to environments where the work is genuinely complex: research institutions, technology organizations, social economy structures. Places where scope is hard to define because the subject matter is dense, the stakeholders are diverse, and the margin for error is real.

French is my second language and also the language in which I do my work. Most of my professional writing, consulting, and client communication has been conducted in French, English or Swahili, where I operate with the highest level of nuance and expression.

I want to build, to solve, to create, with people who bring the same rigor and care to their work.
